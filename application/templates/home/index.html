F.A.Q.

Is there any penalty for canceling the service?

No there's no penalty. You can cancel anytime you want, provided you don't owe us any money.

But since plans are based on yearly contract, if you want to re-enroll, you'll need to pay back the monthly membership fees for remainder of your last contract period.

Should I enroll now even if I don't need to borrow now?

By enrolling early, you protect yourself against unforseen circumstances. When the time comes to borrow, you don't have to do any paperwork, just a text would get you the money.

In any case, if you don't borrow money as a member, part of the membership fees will roll over to the next year.

Is there a contract for the plan?

The plans are based on yearly contract but if you don't like the service you can cancel anytime. You need to be at 0 balance in order to cancel the plan. Once you cancel, you'll loose all benefits. In order to join back, you'll need to pay back any missed monthly membership fee for remainder of the contract period.

What are the criterias for membership?

It's simple - we need proof of employment/paychecks. Users will apply for membership on our site. Once we verify their information, we will provide the application approval decison by the end of the day. In some cases, users may be required to provide additional information such as bank statements, proof of identification etc.

When can I get the money?

Once you make the request, we will transfer the money within 1 business day.
